Crystal Counter

  • At the begening of the game you will be given a random magic number.
  • There are four crystals below, by clicking one of the crystals you will add a specific number of points to your score.
  • If you can match your score to the magic number, you win!
  • If your score goes over the magic number, you lose!
  • The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. The value of the crystals change each game.
